National Service Scheme (NSS) is a voluntary organisation launched in the Gandhiji’s birth century year,1969. The programme aimed to promote students’ personality development by the means of community service. Starting from a handful of 37 universities, today it has got more than 3.2 million student volunteers from all over the country.

About NSS IIT KGP

In IIT-KGP, NSS has been made a part of core academic curriculum for about one third of the first and second year undergraduate students. The programme comprises of weekly village activities and a minimum of one compulsory annual camp. Insight of our activities-
Regular activities-
• Education- From each unit, a teaching team is selected who give weekly tutuions to village school children or children of poor workers in the campus.
• Cleanliness and Environment preservation- Tree plantation, garbage picking, purification of water resourses,’tripal’ distribution for rain water conservation and waste management through indegenious techniques.
• Spreading Awareness- Spreading general awareness on issues of health and hygiene by organising rallies, street play or ‘nukkad’ etc.
• Not cash but ‘Kind’ donation- Distribution of clothing and apt books to village residents.
